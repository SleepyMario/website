So some people might be wondering, 'Why on earth add another project on top of your endless list while they don't allow you to do anything anyway?'

## Why Vietnamese

There might be other questions, for example: Why Vietnamese? I think this particular question is more interesting.

### History

First of all, I'm sure there are interesting things to read. We all understand what, how, why and whatnot, right?

### Proof of Concept

Secondly, and more importantly, this is a proof of concept. Let's just see if I can get some form of communication skills going on in this particular language.

### Linguistic Interest

Third of all, I learned Mandarin before, and fairly successfully. It's hard to explain, but as a Westerner, you wonder how people talk using those strange sounds. I mean, I did learn Mandarin, so I know it's possible, but trust me, the confusion is endless at times. It's the same right now, so let's just figure it out, right?

### What I discovered: Speed

I'm learning this much quicker than I learned Mandarin. In a way, it's easier. Five, six or seven tones don't matter at all anymore. It's still hard, of course, but I don't even bother caring about that. It seems to be going much quicker, so as this is a proof of concept, let's just see how well one can get at this.
